Art Engineering · Season 2

The City
Remembers Itself

Year 2026 Medium Printed acrylic panels on mirrored base, tiered in sequence Size 180 × 80 × 60 cm

The first layer is the city before it was ruined. The last layer is the city ruined. In between are the steps we took to get from one to the other — gradual, unnoticed at the time, obvious in retrospect.

Every visitor walks around it to find the order they want to read it in.

· · ·
Materials
UV-printed clear acrylic panels, mirrored MDF base, brass supports
Panels
Nine, each 6 mm, spaced at 25 mm
Source photography
Composite of present-day Tashkent, archival images, digital decay simulation
Reading
