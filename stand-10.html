Art Engineering · Season 2

One
Choice Apart

Year 2026

This work has two faces. On one side, a healthy green forest still breathes as it has always breathed. On the other, the same place after we have cut and cleared it — stumps, bare soil, a silence that sounds like no wind.

The two sides are not ages apart; they are the same morning, one human choice apart. Seen together, the work does not argue or accuse. It only shows, clearly: a forest is a decision, and we make it every day, even when we think we aren't choosing.

· · ·
Materials
Acrylic panels with surface imprint, two-sided
Faces
Healthy forest on one side, cut and cleared land on the other
Process
Imagery printed directly onto the surface of the acrylic
Viewing
Meant to be walked around — the work completes in motion
