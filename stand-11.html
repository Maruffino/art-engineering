Art Engineering · Season 2

Rust and
Lily

Year 2026

This work speaks through the image of a ship alone in the middle of a desert — a figure for the ecological crisis at its widest scale. In the cracked, exhausted ground, water and life still hide; their presence becomes a symbol of hope.

The ship is a form of rescue — a vessel built to carry life across water. To see it stranded in sand is to see what our treatment of the natural world costs us. The work ends with a quiet argument: it is not yet too late to save what remains alive.

· · ·
Materials
Salvaged steel pipes, coral-reef imitations, live fish, pond basin
Structure
Welded pipes arranged to form a reef
Habitat
Artificial reef supporting live aquatic fish
Process
Pipes cut and welded; coral imitations and water life added afterward
Edition
One of one — living installation
