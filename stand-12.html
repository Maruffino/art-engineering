Art Engineering · Season 2

A Wall of
Accords

Year 2026

A signature is the shortest story we tell about responsibility. It says: on this date, in this room, under this light, I accept that what follows is my burden. The treaties on this wall are crowded with such signatures. The weather outside will decide whether we meant them.

An accord is easier to sign than to keep. This wall reminds us that the hard work of climate commitment is not the ceremony but the daily, boring, unwatched keeping of a promise made in brighter rooms. Every page is also a question: would we sign it again today, knowing what we know now? Every page answers: we must.
