The River That
Was Here

Year 2026 Medium Video projection (4K, silent), black steel pillars, floor treatment Size Dimensions variable — approx. 6 × 4 m footprint

There was a river here once. Not in this room — the room is newer than that — but in the land under it, a watercourse ran east and bent and widened into a flood plain.

The projection shows it still running. The pillars mark where it turned. Visitors walk across the water without getting wet, which was never the way it worked when the river was here.
