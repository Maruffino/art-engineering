Art Engineering · Season 2

The Music of
Motion

Year 2026

An interactive multimedia system that bridges computer vision and audio performance. Using an RTSP camera stream and real-time motion detection (Background Subtraction), the system allows users to "play" a digital instrument by interacting with physical zones.

The project features a synchronized "falling notes" visualizer (rhythm-game style) driven by a customized timeline, creating an immersive, gamified musical experience.

· · ·
Materials
RTSP camera, motion-detection software, audio synthesizer, floor projection system
Technology
Real-time motion detection by background subtraction
Interaction
Visitors' movements across physical zones trigger notes
Visualization
Rhythm-game-style falling notes on a custom timeline
