Art Engineering · Season 2

The
Wounds of the City

Year 2026

This work shows what a modern city keeps from view. Air heavy with what cannot be seen. Waste beyond the edges of the photograph. Noise that never quiets. Green spaces that shrink one year at a time.

The piece is not a protest. It is a portrait, careful enough to hold each harm without flinching. Its argument is slow: that a city could be built differently, if we asked of it less weight and more breath. A sustainable city is not a separate project — it is the daily decision to stop adding to this list.
