Inheritance

Year 2026

An inheritance is the one thing that cannot be refused. A child does not choose whether to be born into summer or into smoke, into freshwater or into plastic. The decision is made, decades in advance, by people the child will never meet.

This piece shows the transaction literally: a planet, a body, and a tape between them like an umbilical cord. What passes through that tape is not love or history. It is consequence. The question the work leaves open is whether we are still in time to decide what consequence we pass on — or whether the decision is already sealed.

· · ·
Materials
Recycled cellophane wrapping, polyurethane tape, inflatable world globe, MDF panel
Subject
A human embryo at approximately eight weeks; the planet at an uncertain age
Process
Cellophane layered wet over a wire armature, then heat-sealed
Dedication
For children who will not have asked for this
Edition
One of one
